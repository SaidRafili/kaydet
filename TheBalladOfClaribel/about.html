About Me

Hello! My name is Whitney, and I'm the eccentric future librarian whose obsession with Claribel spawned this site.

This site was initially created for LIS-S501, my reference librarianship class at IUPUI. However, it's taken on a life beyond that, and I'm in the process of making it my hub for Claribel information

So who on earth was Claribel, anyway? If you're wondering this, you've come to the right place. If you've previously heard me yammering on about this woman and her frankly incredible story, you're also in the right place, and hopefully this site tells you something I forgot to mention. Regardless, feel free to go forth and learn things!
